About Us

Panucci's has been a family owned restaurant in Windsor since 1994. Since then, we've always strived to use fresh ingredients and to serve the tastiest pizza we can deliver.

We cater to a wide range of diets, including a large selection of vegan alternatives to our classic options. Try our in-house restaurant service or have your pizza delivered, either way, enjoy our authentic, delicious Windsor-style pizza today.
